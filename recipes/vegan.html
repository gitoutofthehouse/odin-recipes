Vegan Cashew Milk-Braised Green Plantains

We Love these Plantains with Creamy Cashew Sauce

Dense and meaty green plantains are hearty enough to stand on their own in this creamy cashew sauce. We simmer the thick slabs of plantain in a blend of rich cashew milk, bright herbs, and aromatic spices for balanced flavor and a vibrant hue. Although it's substantial enough as a main course, its bright acidity makes this the perfect side to grilled fish or saut√©ed shrimp as well.

Ingredients

  • 3 large unripe green plantains
  • kosher salt
  • 1/3 cup raw cashews
  • 4 scallions
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro, roughly chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • One 1/2 inch piece fresh turmeric, cut into 1/8 inch matchsticks
  • One 1/2 inch piece fresh ginger, cut into 1/8 inch matchsticks
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teapsoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice, from about 1 lime
  • cooked white rice, for serving
